Supervision Procedure

A structured approach to research supervision that ensures academic rigor, methodological soundness, and timely completion of degree requirements.

Initial Consultation & Topic Selection

Preliminary meeting to discuss research interests, review potential topics, and assess alignment with my expertise. Students present initial ideas and receive guidance on topic refinement.

Duration: 2-4 weeks

Literature Review & Research Proposal

Comprehensive literature review and development of research proposal including research questions, methodology, and timeline. Regular meetings to review progress and provide feedback.

Duration: 3-6 months

Proposal Defense & Ethics Approval

Formal presentation of research proposal to supervisory committee, incorporation of feedback, and obtaining necessary ethics approvals for data collection.

Duration: 1-2 months

Data Collection & Analysis

Implementation of research methodology, data collection, and preliminary analysis. Regular supervision meetings to monitor progress and address methodological challenges.

Duration: 6-12 months

Thesis Writing & Revision

Systematic writing of thesis chapters with iterative feedback and revision process. Focus on academic writing quality, argument development, and presentation of findings.

Duration: 6-9 months

Final Defense & Graduation

Preparation for thesis defense, mock presentations, and final examination. Support through the defense process and post-graduation career guidance.

Duration: 1-2 months
