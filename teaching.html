Teaching

Fostering critical thinking and analytical skills through engaging pedagogy, innovative teaching methods, and comprehensive course materials in economics and political science.

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centers on creating an inclusive and intellectually stimulating environment where students can develop both theoretical understanding and practical analytical skills. I believe that effective learning occurs when students are actively engaged with the material, encouraged to think critically, and provided with real-world applications of economic concepts.

I employ a variety of pedagogical approaches including interactive lectures, case study discussions, data analysis workshops, and collaborative research projects. My goal is to help students not only master economic theory but also develop the quantitative and analytical skills necessary for success in academia, policy work, and the private sector.

Teaching Approach

My pedagogical approach combines traditional economic theory with modern analytical tools and real-world applications to create a comprehensive learning experience.

🎯

Active Learning

Interactive lectures with real-time polling, group discussions, and problem-solving exercises that engage students and promote deeper understanding of economic concepts.

📊

Data-Driven Analysis

Hands-on experience with statistical software and real datasets to develop practical skills in data analysis, visualization, and econometric modeling.

🌍

Global Perspective

Case studies from diverse countries and contexts to illustrate economic principles and help students understand the global nature of economic phenomena.

🔬

Research Integration

Incorporation of current research findings and methodologies into coursework, exposing students to cutting-edge developments in the field.

💬

Critical Thinking

Emphasis on developing analytical and critical thinking skills through debates, policy analysis, and evaluation of competing economic theories.

🤝

Collaborative Learning

Group projects and peer learning opportunities that foster teamwork skills and diverse perspectives on economic issues.
