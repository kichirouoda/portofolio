Research Areas

Our interdisciplinary approach covers key areas in political economy and institutional analysis

🏛️ Democratic Institutions

Analyzing the effectiveness of democratic institutions, electoral systems, and their impact on policy outcomes and economic development.

📊 Governance and Performance

Examining the relationship between governance quality, institutional capacity, and public service delivery in developing countries.

💼 Political Economy of Development

Investigating how political factors influence economic policies, growth patterns, and development outcomes across different contexts.

🌐 Decentralization Studies

Studying fiscal decentralization, local government autonomy, and their effects on regional development and public goods provision.

⚖️ Institutional Economics

Exploring how formal and informal institutions shape economic behavior, market outcomes, and long-term economic performance.

🔍 Policy Analysis

Conducting rigorous policy evaluation and impact assessment using advanced econometric methods and experimental designs.
